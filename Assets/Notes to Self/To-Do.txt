--Skill Tree System--
	- Spend coin earned from battles to buy permanent power up
	- Skill tree is divided into offense, defense, elements, utilities, etc...

--Stage System--
	- After defeating the floor boss, enemies stops spawing
	- Once the remaining enemies have been cleared, door to next floor opens
	- After clearing several floors the door to final boss would spawn

--Element System--
	- Elements are different from the normal upgrades, they are much more powerful and they alter your projectile properties
	- After beating a floor boss, you get to choose a random element to imbue your magic
	- Certain upgrades only shows up on certain elements (eg, explode radius for fire element, etc...)

--Achievement System--
	- Earning Achievements allows you to unlock more upgrades, elements, (maybe characters)
